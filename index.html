Our mission

Elite college and career guidance shouldn't depend on your zip code or your family's network. We're making it free for everyone.

Mentorist replaces that gap with real mentors and an AI Strategy Engine grounded in real data — so any student with drive can compete.

Questions

Good to know

Is Mentorist really free?

Yes. Mentorist is a nonprofit. Students never pay, and there are no ads. Our goal is to make high-quality guidance accessible to everyone.

How does the AI find real courses and internships?

The AI Strategy Engine reads your onboarding quiz and searches real, current sources — like your school's course catalog or open internship postings — then tailors recommendations to your profile. When live sources aren't reachable, it still delivers a high-quality structured plan so you're never left with an error.

Who can join?

Any student from roughly middle school through college. Whether you're getting a head start in 6th grade or hunting for your first internship as an undergrad, there's a path for you.

How do mentors get approved?

Mentors complete a short guided application. Our team reviews each one before approval to keep the community safe and high-quality.
