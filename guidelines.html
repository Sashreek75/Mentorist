Community Guidelines

A simple code for safe, strategic mentorship.

Mentorist is built as a nonprofit network for students and mentors to share guidance, questions, and opportunities. These guidelines keep the space constructive, respectful, and useful for everyone.

Be Respectful

Keep messages constructive. No harassment, hate speech, threats, or personal attacks. We expect everyone to treat others with basic respect.

Give Helpful Guidance

Mentor advice should be honest, specific, and actionable. Students should ask focused questions so mentors can give meaningful help.

Protect Privacy

Do not share sensitive personal information publicly. Only share what is necessary to get and give academic guidance.

No Spam or Abuse

Broadcasts, questions, and replies should be relevant to the Mentorist community. Spam, scams, or deceptive content are not allowed.

Mentor Standards

  • Mentors should only apply if they can provide genuine experience and support.
  • Answers should be thoughtful and not misleading.
  • Rejected mentors may reapply only after the app allows it.

Platform Integrity

  • Do not attempt to bypass account restrictions or admin decisions.
  • Do not impersonate another user.
  • Use the site for its intended educational purpose.

Questions or concerns?

If you need help, email hello@mentorist.org. We review community issues with care and try to keep the platform safe for students and mentors.
